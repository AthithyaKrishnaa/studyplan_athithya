AI

1.1 What is AI?
It is a field of computer science that is capable of building system that perform tasks that usually require human intelligence.

1.2 AI Features
* Logical
* Reasoning
* Problem solving
* Understanding Language
* Recognizing images/videos
* Making decisions

1.3 Types of AI
i. ANI - Artificial Narrow Intelligence (weak AI)
Specialized in a particular area (Ex: ChatGPT - Conversation AI)
ii. AGI - Artificial General Intelligence (strong AI)
Can mimic the human intelligence completely (Ex: coding, driving, cooking, etc)
iii. ASI - Artificial Super Intelligence
Can beat human intelligence in every way possible, still as a theory (Ex: Skynet from Terminator)

1.4 Machine Learning
A subset of AI, that usually learns patterns from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.

1.5 Types of ML
i. Supervised Learning
ii. Unsupervised Learning
iii. Reinforcement Learning
iv. Self-supervised Learning
v. Semi-supervised Learning

1.6 Deep Learning
A subset of ML, which uses the concept of Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) that has multiple hidden layers to learn patterns from large number of datasets. It is used for image/video/speech recognition, NLP and autonomous systems.

1.7 Gen AI
A subset of DL, that is capable of creating text, image, video, speech and code based on the user prompts.
